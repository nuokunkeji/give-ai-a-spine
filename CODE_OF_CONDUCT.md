# Community conduct

Spine exists to make disagreement more useful. Challenge claims and decisions with evidence; do not attack people, assign motives as facts, shame vulnerability, harass contributors, or use identity-based insults.

Good review behavior:

- State the claim you dispute.
- Quote the evidence or missing evidence.
- Offer a falsifiable test or concrete revision.
- Update your position when the evidence changes.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove abusive content and may restrict participation when necessary to keep the project safe and productive. Report conduct concerns through the repository's private security reporting channel.
